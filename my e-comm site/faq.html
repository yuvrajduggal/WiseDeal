How do I know my order has been confirmed?

Once you have successfully placed your order, we will notify you via text message and an email will be sent out to you with the order number. We will also notify you when the order is shipped, and an email is sent out with the tracking information. You can also opt for Whatsapp notification at the time of placing orders to get all the updates about your order directly on your Whatsapp.

How much do you charge for the shipping?

There is very nominal charge of ₹99 as shipping fee for the orders below ₹990. All orders above ₹990 will be free of shipping charges.

How can I track my order?

We strive to deliver the complete order that you have placed. However, sometimes due to high demand for a product, it gets out of stock and if any item from your order has been cancelled then it could be the possible reason. Please reach out to our customer delight team for more clarification either through Chat or email us at email@example.com

Can I change/modify my shipping address?

Yes, you can change your address before the order is shipped depending on the availability of services to your revised location. If we do not have services in your location, we would not be able to change the address. Please contact our support team at email@example.com for if you wish to change the address before the order is shipped. Please note, once the order is shipped, we will not be change anything.

How can I get the invoice for my order?

We generally send the invoice for your order in the package itself. However, in case you have not received it or misplaced it, please contact our Customer Delight team and they would be able to provide you with the invoice copy.
